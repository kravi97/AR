To use MindAR image tracking, you need to create a compiled .mind file from your business card image. This requires using the MindAR compiler.

Step 1: Use Online MindAR Compiler

Visit the MindAR Image Target Compiler to convert your business card image into a trackable target:

Open MindAR Compiler

Step 2: Upload Your Business Card Image

Requirements for best AR tracking:

  • Use a clear, high-quality image of the business card
  • Recommended size: 1024x1024 pixels or similar
  • Good contrast and distinctive features work best
  • Avoid plain white or highly reflective cards

Step 3: Compile and Download

After uploading your image to the compiler:

  • Click "Start" to compile the image
  • Wait for the compilation to complete
  • Download the generated targets.mind file

Step 4: Add to Your Project

Place the downloaded targets.mind file in your assets folder:

C:\ARNEW\assets\targets.mind

Alternative: Use AR.js Pattern Marker

If you prefer to use a simpler pattern-based marker instead:

  • Print the pattern marker included in your assets folder
  • Attach it to your business card
  • Use the pattern-based AR mode (already configured)
Create Custom Pattern
